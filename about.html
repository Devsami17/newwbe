About Us

Since 1994 MyGovernmentContracts.com MGC has helped companies located across the U.S. and around the world successfully win and manage more than Over $20,000,000.00 in Government Contracts. We have a long history of delivering high-quality service and advice that helps our clients accelerate and sustain long-term success–many who have put their trust in our advice for over 29 years. Our Clients represent the full spectrum of industries and business classification from start-ups to multi-billion dollar conglomerates.

Our Mission

MyGovernmentContractor, established in 1994, is dedicated to providing top-tier consultation services for government contracts. Our mission is to empower businesses by offering expert guidance, strategic insights, and comprehensive support to navigate the complexities of government procurement processes. We strive to foster transparency, efficiency, and excellence, ensuring our clients achieve their goals while adhering to the highest standards of compliance and integrity. At MyGovernmentContractor, we are committed to facilitating successful partnerships between businesses and government agencies, driving innovation, and contributing to the public good.

Our Vision

Our vision at MyGovernmentContractor is to be the leading authority in government consultation, recognized for our unwavering commitment to excellence and innovation. We aspire to transform the landscape of government contracting by leveraging our expertise to create seamless and efficient procurement experiences for businesses of all sizes. By continually adapting to evolving industry standards and embracing cutting-edge technologies, we aim to set new benchmarks in the field. Our ultimate goal is to empower our clients to thrive in the competitive government market, fostering sustainable growth and fostering lasting positive impacts on the communities we serve.

Our Purpose

At MyGovernmentContractor, our purpose is to bridge the gap between businesses and government opportunities. We are dedicated to guiding and supporting enterprises in navigating the intricate government contracting landscape, ensuring they have the resources and knowledge to succeed. By providing tailored consultation services, we help businesses unlock their potential, drive economic growth, and contribute to public sector advancements. Our purpose is rooted in a commitment to excellence, integrity, and innovation, as we work tirelessly to facilitate successful collaborations that benefit both our clients and the broader community.

Our Values

At MyGovernmentContractor, our values are the bedrock of our commitment to providing exceptional government consultation services. We adhere to the highest standards of integrity, ensuring transparency and trust in all our interactions. Striving for excellence, we deliver top-quality services and achieve outstanding results for our clients. Embracing innovation, we continuously seek out new and effective solutions to meet the evolving needs of our clients and the industry. Our client-centric approach places our clients' unique needs at the forefront, allowing us to deliver personalized and impactful solutions.
