MGC Broker Training Course

Unlock the full potential of government contracting with our Broker Training. Master the essential skills to navigate contracts, scale your business, and achieve success in the set-aside marketplace. Join our expert-led course and elevate your business to new heights. Get started today and secure your place in the competitive government contracts arena.

We are excited to announce our upcoming Government Broker Courses, designed to enhance your certification and support your business endeavors.

I would like to summarize the key points of the GCAS Broker training course. This comprehensive six-step system guides your client through all phases of government contracting within the set-aside marketplace, emphasizing that 23% of all contracts are mandated to be set aside for small businesses, which is our target market.

Here is what you can expect to learn during the training:

  1. How to register your client for municipal, state, and federal contracting opportunities.
  2. How to select contracts to place with your prospects.
  3. How to read solicitations and immediately identify the requirements to match the contract with the right company.
  4. The one-call close technique, ensuring you receive your retainer fee and a signed agreement after your conversation.
  5. The importance of the capability statement and how to write one on behalf of your client.
  6. How to select and qualify your client’s company for an SBA certification.
  7. How to broker and scale your client’s company through government contracts within a tri-state area.
  8. How to access government funding through the SBA and the SBIC.
  9. How to interview and qualify your prospect.
  10. The purpose of the interview script and your consulting agreement with your client.
  11. How to structure your client’s folder for a comprehensive understanding of their position with a click of a mouse.
  12. A detailed step-by-step methodology to onboard your client, consult with them, and grow their company while documenting every transaction.
  13. How to transfer data in real-time to provide your client with the best possible conversion rates.
  14. How to manage your clients’ expectations and growth patterns with documentation.
  15. How to hire and train an administrative assistant and the importance of this role.
  16. How to subcontract through the Subnet portal via the SBA.
  17. How to scale your client’s company through RFPs, team arrangements, and joint ventures.
  18. How to work in multiple time zones and industries simultaneously.
  19. Upon completing our training, you will be well-informed and equipped with the necessary skills to scale your business full-time.
  20. How to onboard 100 clients within your first six months.
  21. How to secure companies for you to call on through our tried, tested, and proven marketing techniques, at no cost for leads. We teach you how to get these leads for free.
