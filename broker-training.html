MGC Broker Training Course

Unlock the full potential of government contracting with our Broker Training. Master the essential skills to navigate contracts, scale your business, and achieve success in the set-aside marketplace. Join our expert-led course and elevate your business to new heights. Get started today and secure your place in the competitive government contracts arena.

Please allow me to summarize what you just heard. The GCAS Broker training course is comprised of a six-step system that walks your client through all phases of government contracting relating to the set aside marketplace to reiterate 23% of all contracts are mandated to be set aside for small businesses, this is our target market. Here is what you can expect to learn during Training:

  1. How to register your client for municipal, state and federal contracting opportunities.
  2. How to select contracts to place with your prospects.
  3. How to read the solicitation and immediately pick out the requirements so you can match up the contract with the right company.
  4. You will learn the one call close by after your conversation you will receive your retainer fee as well as a signed agreement.
  5. You will learn the Importance of the capability statement and how to write one behalf of your client
  6. You will learn how to select and qualify your client's company for an SBA certification.
  7. We teach you how to Broker & scale your client's company through GOVERNMENT CONTRACTS within a tri-state area.
  8. We teach you how to access government funding through the SBA and the SBIC.
  9. We teach you how to interview and qualify your prospect.
  10. We teach you the interview script's purpose and your consulting agreement with your client.
  11. We teach you how to structure your client's folder, so you have a comprehensive understanding of where your client position is with a click of a mouse.
  12. You will learn in detail the step-by-step methodology to onboard your client, consult with your client and grow your client's company while documenting every transaction.
  13. We teach you how to transfer data in real time to give your client the best possible conversion rates.
  14. We teach you how to manage your clients' expectations and growth patterns with documentation.
  15. We teach you how to hire & train an administrative assistant and the importance of that.
  16. We teach you how to subcontract through the subnet portal through the SBA.
  17. We teach you how to scale your client's company through RFP's and Team arrangements are joint ventures.
  18. We teach you how to work in multiple time zones and multiple industries simultaneously.
  19. When you complete our training, you will be very well informed, and have the necessary skill set to scale your business full-time.
  20. Learn how to onboard 100 clients in less than six months.
